Biography

I am a life-long learner and Full Stack Application Developer. I specialize in solving business work-flow problems through the UI experience, report design and thoughtful back-end database development. I'm detail oriented. My passion and goals in business are to provide meaningful, useful, timely information to management and business users. The most common report issues stem from inaccurate data so I focus on solving the data-entry pain points to minimize the struggle of obtaining timely accurate data.

Before moving into the IT field, I held positions in finance, accounting and business intelligence. Because of my business experience I often fulfill the role of liaison between business and technical departments. My strong analytical and detail oriented nature are skills that combine well with technology to help business owners and department managers maximize efficiency.

Currently enrolled in UCSD web-development boot camp course to learn the MEAN stack and improve existing technical skills. I develop in WINS (Windows Server, IIS Web Server, SQL Server, and PHP) stack environment for my current employer.

When I'm not learning a new coding language, I like to spend time with my two dogs, two cats and one husband. I enjoy the arts, our local zoo and sharing the many tourist attractions that can be found throughout San Diego with family and friends.
